In the Verkiai regional park where the trail is located the total number of plant species is 870, of which 3 are protected by the European Communities, 13 are listed in the Lithuanian Red Book. The total number of animal species - 195. Mammals - 40, of which 4 are protected by the European Communities, 6 are listed in the Lithuanian Red Book. Birds - 126, of which 6 are protected by European Communities, 15 are listed in the Lithuanian Red Book. There are 19 fish, of which 1 is protected by the European Community. Amphibians and reptiles - 10, of which 1 is protected by the European Community, 2 are listed in the Lithuanian Red Book.
